Cheese Pizza

Original Recipe Source

Ingredients

1 pound pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)

1/2 cup pizza sauce

1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

1 1/2 teaspoons Italian seasoning

Instructions

Preheat your oven to 500Â°F. If available, place a pizza stone inside during preheating.

On a floured surface, roll out the pizza dough to create a 12-inch circle.

Transfer the rolled dough onto a pizza tray.

Evenly spread the pizza sauce across the dough.

Sprinkle parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, and Italian seasoning evenly over the sauce.

Place the pizza on the preheated pizza stone in the oven.

Bake until the cheese has melted and slightly browned, about 10 to 15 minutes.
